Arizona

The Care Ranch in Arizona

Set in the high Sonoran Desert of Arizona, The Care Ranch collaborates with the historic Tubac Ranch — built in the 1800s — preserving its essence and partnership with nature.

The ranch is restored with care, respect, and restraint, honoring the landscape and its historic charm.

Nothing is excessive or distracting from the transformative journey.

The land is open. The rhythm is slow. The silence is your peace.

Here, simplicity calms the nervous system while the absence of excess reduces cognitive load. This historic space slows perception and grounds the body. Nothing here is accidental.

Food is prepared with care, using whole seasonal ingredients that support the nervous system and reflect the rhythm of the land.

Nature is not a backdrop — it is an active force.

It regulates. It slows perception. It brings back what is essential.

The desert does not add anything. It removes.

And in that space, vision becomes visible again.

Method

The Care Ranch Method

Grounded in neuroscience and refined through practice, we reveal great leadership by cultivating a relationship with the body and its nervous system.

How you lead and decide is shaped by how your system operates — especially under pressure.

We develop leadership by restoring alignment in the body and nervous system, allowing for clear perception and more precise decision-making.

This is one integrated, multilayered approach — working through multiple entry points.

Movement and Body Alignment

Working with posture, coordination, and movement patterns to reorganize neuromuscular pathways and restore alignment between body and brain.

Equine Facilitated Learning

Horses respond to what is real — not what is intended. They reflect incongruence in timing, presence, and boundaries instantly, making underlying patterns visible without interpretation.

Neuro-Kinesiology

Using muscle biofeedback to identify neurological stress patterns and bring unconscious responses into awareness. From there, the system reorganizes.

Coaching and EMDR

Individual one-on-one coaching designed for reflection and integration, supporting the processing of underlying patterns and translating insight into concrete decisions and action. Where relevant, EMDR is used to help shift deeply rooted patterns, allowing new perspectives and responses to emerge.

Nature and Physiology

The environment, the rhythm of the day, and the nourishment support regulation, recovery, and integration at a physiological level.

Together, this creates a shift:

From thinking to sensing.
From reacting to responding.
From fragmentation to alignment.

At the center lies what we call the Wheel of Congruence — where vision, emotion, body, and action come back into alignment.

You learn to read your body and emotions as information. Not something to push through, but something that informs direction.

Experience

The Experience

The experience unfolds over six days — a rhythm of activation and integration. Structured, yet spacious.

Mornings begin in the body: movement, alignment, regulation. During the day, the work deepens — equine sessions, neuro-kinesiology, and coaching. Always alternating between experience, reflection, and integration.

Late afternoons create space for reflection and sauna house experiences. Evenings settle under a sky filled with stars.

Each participant arrives with their own question. Sometimes clear. Sometimes not yet fully formed.

Private sessions create precision. The group — intentionally small — creates reflection and perspective.

Journey

The Journey

Preparing for the Experience

The journey begins before arrival. Each participant starts with an intake questionnaire and session, exploring context, key questions, and underlying patterns.

A preparation guide supports participants in:

  • Clarifying intention
  • Increasing awareness
  • Slowing down before entering the program

This ensures participants arrive focused and connected to what is ready to be explored.

Six-Day Immersive Leadership Experience

Participants enter an environment designed to regulate the nervous system. As the system settles:

  • Perception sharpens
  • Internal noise reduces
  • Patterns become visible

Through Equine Facilitated Learning, neuro-kinesiology, body alignment, coaching, and nature-based practices, insight is not only understood — it is experienced and embodied.

Integration — Sustaining Change

After the immersion, the process continues.

Participants are supported in translating insights into daily life and professional context through a structured integration phase, including follow-up sessions and integration guidance.

The focus is on:

  • Stabilizing new patterns
  • Maintaining clarity under pressure
  • Embedding change over time

Because transformation is not defined by the experience itself — but by what remains.
